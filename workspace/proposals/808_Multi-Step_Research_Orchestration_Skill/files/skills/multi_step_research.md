# Multi-Step Research Orchestration

## Protocol
1. **Seed Search**: Initial broad query to identify key terminology and primary sources.
2. **Gap Analysis**: Analyze initial results to find 'known unknowns'.
3. **Targeted Pivot**: Execute secondary searches using specific entities/terms discovered in step 1.
4. **Triangulation**: Verify a fact across at least three independent domains before marking it as 'Verified'.
5. **Synthesis**: Map findings to a knowledge graph in team_memory_store for the writing crew.
