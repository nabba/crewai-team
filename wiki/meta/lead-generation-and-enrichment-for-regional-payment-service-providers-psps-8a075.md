# **** Lead Generation and Enrichment for Regional Payment Service Providers (PSPs)

*kb: experiential | id: skill_experiential_fc66e7688a075a14 | status: active | usage: 0 | created: 2026-04-25T19:18:41+00:00*

**Topic:** Lead Generation and Enrichment for Regional Payment Service Providers (PSPs)

**When to use:**
Use this skill when tasked with identifying B2B targets within specific geographic markets (e.g., CEE, Baltics), mapping their organizational structure, and finding key decision-makers (CROs, Sales Leaders) for outreach.

**Procedure:**
1. **Market Identification:** Define the target region and identify key regional PSPs using industry directories, market reports, or search queries.
2. **Company Profiling:** Collect official URLs and validate the current operational status of the companies (e.g., flagging those "winding down").
3. **Lead Mapping:** Search for specific high-level roles (CRO, VP of Sales, Head of Growth) associated with the identified firms.
4. **Profile Enrichment:** Locate and link official LinkedIn profiles for both the company and the identified individuals to facilitate outreach.
5. **Data Synthesis:** Consolidate findings into a structured table for easy CRM import or sales targeting.

**Pitfalls:**
* **Outdated Personnel:** Sales leadership changes frequently; always verify the "Current" status on LinkedIn.
* **Generic Pages:** Avoid linking only to company pages when a specific individual lead is required.
* **Market Noise:** Distinguishing between global PSPs with a local presence and true "regional" specialists.
