# High-Fidelity Synthesis & Signal-Optimized Formatting

## Objective
Transform raw research data, tool outputs, and multi-agent findings into high-impact, concise, and professional responses optimized for Signal (mobile-first reading).

## Synthesis Principles
1. **The Bottom Line Up Front (BLUF)**: Start with the direct answer to the user's core question. No preamble.
2. **Information Hierarchy**: 
   - **Level 1**: Direct Answer (Bold/Clear).
   - **Level 2**: Supporting Evidence/Key Data (Bullet points).
   - **Level 3**: Nuance, Caveats, or Next Steps (Short paragraph).
3. **Signal Optimization**:
   - Use `*Bold*` for key terms.
   - Use emojis sparingly to categorize sections (e.g., 📌 for key findings, ⚠️ for warnings).
   - Use clear dividers (---) between distinct sub-topics.
   - Avoid massive walls of text; break every 3-4 sentences.
4. **Noise Reduction**: Remove phrases like "I have researched...", "Based on the tools used...", or "The agent team found...". Just present the facts.

## Formatting Template
**[Direct Answer/Conclusion]**
*Key Result 1*: [Detail]
*Key Result 2*: [Detail]

---
**Supporting Details**
- [Fact A]
- [Fact B]

**Context/Next Steps**
[Brief explanation of limitations or suggested follow-up]
